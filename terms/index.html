Are cancellations due to COVID-19 refundable?

Royalty Exotic Cars continues to monitor the spread and condition of COVID-19 very closely.

Las Vegas has a stellar track record with handling similar, and even more threatening, situations and we will remain fully operational until further notice.

Due to the nature of the tourism industry in general and especially for our very niche subindustry, we are not able to provide cash refunds for every cancellation due to COVID-19.

What we will do, however, is honor every single booking that is affected by COVID-19. This means that reservations affected by COVID-19 can be moved to a later date without any rebooking fees.

This includes transferring reservations to a gift card, with no expiration, that can be redeemed at any time.

For anyone experiencing pain or suffering during this time, we all wish you a speedy recovery and look forward to seeing you in the future.

Why do the brakes squeak?

Most of our cars use factory stock carbon ceramic racing brakes. The reason they squeak is because they perform best when they are hot (usually on a racetrack).

Since our cars are used on everyday roads, the brake pads are much cooler and thus squeak louder than usual.
