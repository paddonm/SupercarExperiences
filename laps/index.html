Test Drives

Everyone has a bucket list of things they want to achieve or experience in their lives, and if you’ve always wanted to drive a super car, then here’s your chance to make that dream come true. At Supercar Experiences, we offer the chance to drive your favourite car on a race track so that you can get a taste of the speed and power that it has to offer. Our Tier 1 Lap Package lets you enjoy one full lap under safe conditions for an affordable rate that varies depending on the car you choose.

Frequently asked Questions

info@supercarexperiences.ca
Are cancellations due to COVID-19 refundable?

Royalty Exotic Cars continues to monitor the spread and condition of COVID-19 very closely.

Las Vegas has a stellar track record with handling similar, and even more threatening, situations and we will remain fully operational until further notice.

Due to the nature of the tourism industry in general and especially for our very niche subindustry, we are not able to provide cash refunds for every cancellation due to COVID-19.

What we will do, however, is honor every single booking that is affected by COVID-19. This means that reservations affected by COVID-19 can be moved to a later date without any rebooking fees.

This includes transferring reservations to a gift card, with no expiration, that can be redeemed at any time.

For anyone experiencing pain or suffering during this time, we all wish you a speedy recovery and look forward to seeing you in the future.

‍

Are there any guarantees?

Although we have a 99% perfect track record for reservation accuracy; Supercar Experiences reserves the right to cancel any confirmed booking. Should negative weather conditions occur on the day of your rental, we may offer you a full refund or a choice of another vehicle. While we do our best to guarantee availability of all the vehicles in our fleet, please note that your reservation is not legally binding, and we cannot guarantee availability unless confirmed by one of our staff members.

Are there any security deposits?

Yes.

At the time of rental we require a pre-authorized security deposit that ranges from $5,000 to $10,000, depending on the vehicle.

The pre-authorized deposit can be paid via card and/or cash. We accept every major credit card by credit card (prepaid credit cards are not accepted). Note—the pre-authorized security deposit is fully refunded upon return of the vehicle in satisfactory condition.

Are there any taxes?

Yes.

All rental prices are subject to a 13% tax for GST/HST.

How many kilometers do I get with my rental?

Each rental vehicle is allotted a total of 200km per 24 hours.

Are there hourly rentals?

Yes.

Supercar Experiences offers limited-duration tours of our exotic vehicles—just ensure that you are booking a tour when making a reservation online.

Can I pay with cash?

Yes.

Both our rental fee and pre-authorized deposit can be paid via cash and/or credit card.

Do I have to have a drivers license?

Yes.

Minimum renter’s age is 21 years old. Must have a full G certificated licence.

Do I need to arrive early for pickup?

Yes. 

Please ensure to arrive about 30 to 45 minutes prior to your scheduled rental to accommodate any paperwork as well as a walk-through of your rental vehicle.

Do guys offer a drop-off or pick-up service?

Yes. 

Supercar Experiences offers both a drop-off and pick-up service, at a cost of $100.00 CAD for each service. Please call us at 416-900-1102 to speak with one our personnel to arrange your drop-off or pick-up service.

How old do I have to be to rent a car?

Minimum 21 years old.
